# Story Arc 01: The Art Dream

## Overview

Maya enters a school art competition in secret, knowing Dad won't approve. When Tunde discovers her sketchbook, the family confronts what it means to support someone's dreams.

**Theme:** Dreams vs Security / Love expressed as control

**Arc Structure:**
- Episodes 1-3: Maya's perspective
- Episode 4: Tunde's perspective (revelation)

---

## Episode 1A: The Secret

**Perspective:** Maya
**Logline:** Maya learns about an art competition but knows her dad won't understand.

### Scene Breakdown

**SCENE 1 (0:00-0:15) - Hook**
- Location: School corridor
- Characters: Maya, friend (Chloe - offscreen voice)
- Action: Maya sees art competition poster, lingers
- Dialogue: Friend: "You should totally enter, Maya. You're the best artist in our year."
- Shot: Close-up Maya's face, excited but conflicted

**SCENE 2 (0:15-0:35) - The Problem**
- Location: Fashanu kitchen, evening
- Characters: Full family at dinner
- Action: Tunde asks about school, Maya mentions "just normal stuff"
- Dialogue:
  - Tunde: "How was school? Anything important coming up?"
  - Maya: "Just... normal stuff."
  - Rachel notices Maya's hesitation but doesn't push
- Shot: Medium shot of family, focus on Maya's avoidance

**SCENE 3 (0:35-0:55) - The Decision**
- Location: Maya's bedroom, night
- Characters: Maya alone
- Action: Maya looks at the competition flyer, then at her sketchbook
- Internal dialogue (voiceover): "He'll just say it's not a real thing. That I should focus on what matters."
- She starts drawing
- Shot: Close-up hands drawing, then face - determined

**SCENE 4 (0:55-1:10) - Cliffhanger**
- Location: Maya's bedroom door
- Characters: Maya (inside), Rachel (outside)
- Action: Knock on door. Maya quickly hides sketchbook.
- Dialogue: Rachel: "Maya? You've been quiet. Everything okay?"
- Maya: "Fine, Mum. Just tired."
- Shot: Maya's worried face as door opens slightly
- End on: Rachel's suspicious but accepting expression

**End card:** "She had two weeks to keep the secret..."

## Episode 1B: The Work

**Perspective:** Maya
**Logline:** Maya works on her competition piece in secret, but keeping the lie becomes harder.

### Scene Breakdown

**SCENE 1 (0:00-0:15) - Hook**
- Location: Maya's bedroom, late night
- Characters: Maya
- Action: Drawing by lamp light, looks at clock - 11:47 PM
- Voiceover: "I just needed a few more hours each night. No one had to know."
- Shot: Atmospheric, shadows, dedication visible

**SCENE 2 (0:15-0:35) - Close Call**
- Location: Kitchen, morning
- Characters: Maya, Rachel, Tayo
- Action: Maya exhausted at breakfast, almost falls asleep
- Dialogue:
  - Rachel: "Maya, you look shattered. What time did you sleep?"
  - Maya: "Normal time."
  - Tayo: "I heard her moving around really late—"
  - Maya: (sharp) "Mind your own business, Tayo."
- Shot: Rachel's concern, Tayo's hurt expression

**SCENE 3 (0:35-0:55) - The Ally**
- Location: Rachel's home office corner
- Characters: Rachel alone, then Maya appears
- Action: Rachel working, pauses, remembers something
- Voiceover (Rachel): "I remember what it's like to want something no one else understands."
- She doesn't confront Maya. Just watches.
- Shot: Rachel looking toward Maya's room door

**SCENE 4 (0:55-1:10) - The Discovery**
- Location: Living room
- Characters: Tunde, finds sketchbook on sofa
- Action: Tunde sits down, notices corner of sketchbook under cushion
- He pulls it out. Opens it.
- His face: unreadable.
- Shot: Close-up on the drawings - they're beautiful
- End on: Tunde's expression shifts

**End card:** "He wasn't supposed to find it."

## Episode 1C: The Confrontation

**Perspective:** Maya
**Logline:** Tunde confronts Maya about the art competition. Old wounds open.

### Scene Breakdown

**SCENE 1 (0:00-0:15) - Hook**
- Location: Living room
- Characters: Maya enters, sees Tunde holding sketchbook
- Action: She freezes
- Dialogue: Tunde: "Sit down, Maya."
- Shot: Maya's fear, Tunde's controlled face

**SCENE 2 (0:15-0:40) - The Clash**
- Location: Living room
- Characters: Tunde, Maya. Rachel in doorway.
- Dialogue:
  - Tunde: "An art competition. Behind my back."
  - Maya: "Because I knew you'd say no!"
  - Tunde: "I'm trying to help you think about your future—"
  - Maya: "This IS my future! Why can't you see that?"
  - Rachel: "Tunde, maybe we should—"
  - Tunde: "Rachel, please."
- Shot: Tennis match between father and daughter

**SCENE 3 (0:40-0:55) - The Break**
- Location: Living room
- Characters: Maya, breaking
- Dialogue:
  - Maya: "You never support anything I actually care about. Never."
  - (Beat)
  - Maya: "Why can't you just be proud of me?"
- She runs out
- Shot: Tunde left alone. Rachel looks at him. Tayo in the hallway, watching.

**SCENE 4 (0:55-1:10) - Aftermath**
- Location: Hallway
- Characters: Tayo alone
- Action: Tayo looks at Maya's closed door. Looks at parents in living room.
- He doesn't know where to go.
- Shot: Small boy caught between two worlds
- End on: His confused, sad face

**End card:** "But Tunde had his own story..."

## Episode 1D: The Father

**Perspective:** Tunde
**Logline:** We learn why Tunde fears Maya's art dream. He once had dreams too.

### Scene Breakdown

**SCENE 1 (0:00-0:15) - Hook**
- Location: Living room (same scene as 1C, different angle)
- Characters: Tunde
- Action: Maya runs out. Tunde stands alone.
- Voiceover (Tunde): "She thinks I don't understand. She doesn't know."
- Shot: Tunde's face - not angry. Broken.

**SCENE 2 (0:15-0:40) - The Memory**
- Location: Flashback - young Tunde (18-ish) in Lagos/France
- Characters: Young Tunde, perhaps with Tiwa
- Voiceover: "I used to draw too. All the time. I was going to be an architect. Create things."
- Flash of sketches. Youthful hope.
- Voiceover: "But life wasn't about dreams where I came from. It was about survival."
- Shot: Quick flashes - boarding school, France, starting over

**SCENE 3 (0:40-0:55) - The Fear**
- Location: Living room, present
- Characters: Tunde alone, then Rachel joins
- Voiceover: "I made a choice. A safe choice. And I've never stopped wondering."
- Dialogue:
  - Rachel: "Tunde... what happened in there?"
  - Tunde: (long pause) "I don't want her to have to wonder like I do."
  - Rachel: "What do you mean?"
- He doesn't answer
- Shot: The gap between them visible

**SCENE 4 (0:55-1:10) - The Turn**
- Location: Hallway outside Maya's door
- Characters: Tunde
- Action: He stands there. Doesn't knock. Holds the sketchbook.
- He looks at her drawings again. Really looks.
- Voiceover: "She's better than I ever was."
- He takes a breath.
- Shot: His hand about to knock
- End on: The door. Possibility.

**End card:** "To be continued..."

## Production Notes

### Character Consistency Keys
- Tunde's outfit: Same across all Ep1 scenes (evening at home - casual shirt)
- Maya's outfit: School uniform in Sc1, home clothes rest
- Location lighting: Consistent warm home tones

### Emotional Beats to Capture
- Maya: Excitement → Stress → Defiance → Hurt
- Tunde: Control → Discovery → Confrontation → Vulnerability
- Rachel: Concern → Knowing → Helpless
- Tayo: Curious → Confused → Scared

### Music Direction
- Ep1A-1C: Tense, building underscore
- Ep1D: Softer, reflective, piano-based
