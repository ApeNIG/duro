# Episode 1A: Image Prompts

Copy these prompts into Gemini, DALL-E, or Bing Image Creator.

---

## Scene 1: School Corridor

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image of an 11 year old mixed race girl (light brown skin, curly shoulder-length hair) in British school uniform, standing in a school corridor looking at a colorful art competition poster on a noticeboard. She has an excited but conflicted expression. Natural school lighting, slightly blurred background showing other students. Shot from medium distance, warm tones.
```

---

## Scene 2: Family Dinner

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image of a mixed race British-Nigerian family of four eating dinner together in a suburban kitchen. Father: 35 year old Nigerian man, dark brown skin, short beard, wearing casual shirt. Mother: 37 year old white British woman, light brown hair tied back. Daughter: 11 year old mixed race girl looking down at her plate avoiding eye contact. Son: 8 year old mixed race boy. Warm evening lighting, cozy kitchen with white cabinets and family photos on fridge. Medium wide shot showing whole table.
```

---

## Scene 3: Maya's Bedroom Night

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image of an 11 year old mixed race girl (light brown skin, curly hair) sitting at a desk in her bedroom at night, drawing in a sketchbook by lamplight. The room has art posters on walls, some art supplies visible. She looks focused and determined. Warm lamp lighting creates shadows, intimate atmosphere. Close-up to medium shot, side profile view.
```

---

## Scene 4: Bedroom Door

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image from inside a teenager's bedroom looking toward a slightly open door. A 37 year old white British woman (light brown hair, concerned expression) is visible in the doorway, checking on her child. Warm hallway light spills through the door crack. Moody, intimate lighting. The perspective is from the bed looking toward the door.
```

## Tips for Consistency

1. Generate character portraits FIRST
2. Save them locally
3. Upload as reference when generating scene images
4. Use "same character as reference image" in prompts
5. Keep lighting consistent (warm home lighting for all indoor scenes)
