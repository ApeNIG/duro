# Episode 1B: Image Prompts

Copy these prompts into image generation tools or use with Pollinations API.

---

## Scene 1: Late Night Work

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image of an 11 year old mixed race girl with light brown skin and curly shoulder-length hair, sitting at her desk in bedroom late at night, drawing intensely in a sketchbook by warm lamp light. Digital clock shows 11:47 PM in the background. Art supplies scattered on desk. The room is dark except for the lamp creating dramatic shadows. She looks tired but determined. Close-up to medium shot, cinematic lighting.
```

## Scene 2: Exhausted Breakfast

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image of a British kitchen in the morning. An 11 year old mixed race girl with curly hair looks exhausted at the breakfast table, struggling to stay awake. A 37 year old white British woman with light brown hair looks at her with concern. An 8 year old mixed race boy sits at the table looking curious. Morning light through window, cereal boxes and toast on table. Medium wide shot showing the family dynamic.
```

## Scene 3: The Ally

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image of a 37 year old white British woman with light brown shoulder-length hair, sitting at a home office corner desk. She has paused her work on a laptop, looking thoughtfully toward a door (off camera). Natural daylight from nearby window. She has a knowing, understanding expression. Cozy home office with plants and personal touches. Medium shot, soft natural lighting.
```

## Scene 4: The Discovery

**Prompt:**
```
Photorealistic image of a 35 year old Nigerian man with dark brown skin and short beard, sitting on a living room sofa holding an open sketchbook. He is looking at beautiful drawings inside with an unreadable, complex expression. Warm evening living room lighting, modern British suburban home interior. Close-up on his face and the sketchbook in his hands.
```

---

## Tips for Consistency

1. Use character reference images from /characters/ folder
2. Maintain warm home lighting throughout
3. Maya should look tired/exhausted in morning scene
4. Tunde's expression in Scene 4 should be complex - not angry, not happy
